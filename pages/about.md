## About Sunset Park Postcards from the Brooklyn Daily Eagle

This page contains a collection of photo postcards published by the Brooklyn Eagle newspaper of locations in what is now Sunset Park, Brooklyn.

These cards were part of a postcard series published by the Eagle from 1905 to 1907.

Out of a total of 486 postcards, published weekly in series of six, 15 are of the Sunset Park area. 

These include many scenes, some that will be familiar to today's viewers, and some of scenes that no longer exist, or that have changed significantly since publication.
